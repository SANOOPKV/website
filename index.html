About

Curry Gurru The Malabar Kitchen

Kerala is one of the state's of south India,called "God's Own Country". Since time memorial, "Malabar", Kerala's spicey garden is famous for its tasty cuisines. We serve authentic Indian and South Indian dishes .The blending of fresh ingredients and the use of traditional home ground recipes brings a unique taste and freshness to our food that you can find only in "The Malabar kitchen", Tay street
